# South East Technological University — Institutional Guidance on Learning Outcomes

SETU employs a centralised **Academic Council Quality Framework** bridging legacy systems of WIT and IT Carlow. Bound by the "Joint-Sectoral Protocol between Designated Awarding Bodies and Quality and Qualifications Ireland."

## Verb Requirements

Internal curriculum review guidelines require teams to:

> "review the programme and module learning outcomes, with a view to enhancing consistency and alignment between the learning outcomes and content while ensuring use of active verbs."

Active verb use is explicitly required as a quality criterion in curriculum review.

## NFQ Alignment

Framework strictly aligns to NFQ levels:
- Level 6: Higher Certificate
- Level 7: Bachelor Degree
- Level 8: Honours Bachelor Degree
- Level 9: Masters Programme

## Curriculum Checklist Requirements

> "that the learning outcomes, and diverse learning experiences, are aligned with the development of in-demand skills and competencies."

## Notes for AI Evaluator

- Active verb is explicitly required in SETU's curriculum review standards
- NFQ level alignment is the primary structural requirement
- SETU is a merged institution (WIT + IT Carlow) — quality may vary across legacy campuses; apply consistent QQI/NFQ baseline
- No dedicated LO writing handbook found beyond what's embedded in curriculum review checklists
- Apply QQI/Cedefop/Kennedy 2006 as the primary evaluative frame where SETU-specific guidance is absent
